Bio

Melanie is living and working in the Republic of Ireland. She holds a BSc in Psychology (Honours) from Arizona State University and an MSc in Psychological Science from the University of Limerick. Key modules included All About Data, Research Methods, Cognitive Science, Stastical Methods, Social Influence, Organisational Psychology, Advanced Statistics, Advanced Research Methods, and a master's thesis using mediation models on big data. Melanie learned R, SQL, and Tableau from the Google Career Certificate in Data Analytics. Melanie has five years' experience working with big data as well as experience in health, academic, and engineering sectors. She is especially interested in data analyst roles within the public sector, health, and sustainable energy. Melanie is an accomplished recording artist with three albums and five radio appearances under her belt. In her free time she enjoys advancing her skills in drop D guitar.
